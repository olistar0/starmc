About us

CozySMP is a Minecraft server made by dedicated long time players of Minecraft. Our goal is to offer a simple vanilla experience, with little additions.

Supporting us is highly appreciated! The majority of the funds are put straight back into the server! A Minecraft server has many cost unfortunately, while we have no problem paying the bills to ensure the best for CozySMP, any help is very appreciated and will help us a lot.

Support

Need any questions answered before checkout? Waited more than 30 minutes but your package still has not arrived? Ask the community/staff on Discord, or for payment support, submit a support ticket on our discord.

If you are player, please join our discord for fast support! CLICK HERE TO JOIN
If you have any questions or concerns, please contact cozysmp.mc@gmail.com

Refund policy

All payments are final and non-refundable. Attempting a chargeback or opening a PayPal dispute will result in permanent and irreversible banishment from all of our servers, and other minecraft stores.

It could take between 1-20 minutes for your purchase to be credited in-game. If you are still not credited after this time period, please open a support ticket on our forums with proof of purchase and we will look into your issue.
